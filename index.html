FAQ

Common questions.

Answers to the questions property managers and asset managers ask before signing.

Will install disrupt tenants or operations?

No. Most sensors are wireless and battery-powered — no cutting, no drilling, no water shutoffs. We schedule by floor during normal business hours. Your facilities team gets a coordination brief before we start.

How many false alarms will we get?

Very few. The system filters by flow rate and duration before alerting, so condensation and humidity spikes don’t trip a call. Real alerts come tagged Informational, Caution, or Critical — with exact sensor location and failure type, not a raw beep.

Does this replace my maintenance team?

No. NestShield runs on top of your existing team. Your staff gets a clean alert with exact location and severity, so reactive emergency calls turn into scheduled dispatches. We onboard your team and give you a written escalation playbook for your SOPs.

Why not just buy sensors off Amazon and do it ourselves?

Sensors alone are detection. They don’t tell you where to put them, when to replace them, or how to package the data for your insurance carrier. NestShield does all of that — site walk, install, staff training, 24/7 monitoring, battery swaps, annual audits, and insurance-ready paperwork. You’re paying for accountability, not parts.

What happens when sensors fail or get old?

We own the hardware. Batteries get replaced on a 5-year schedule. Failed sensors get swapped under warranty. When the technology updates, we upgrade the gateways at no extra cost. You never deal with hardware obsolescence.

How long does install take?

Roughly one day per 100 units. 90%+ of sensors need no permits, no cutting, no utility shutoffs. The optional shutoff valves require brief scheduled water outages — coordinated with your maintenance team.
